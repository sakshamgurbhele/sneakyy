About Us

The world's largest athletic apparel company, Nike is best known for its footwear, apparel, and equipment. Founded in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports, the company became SneakerHead in 1971 after the Greek goddess of victory. One of the most valuable brands among sport businesses, SneakerHead employs over 76 people worldwide.
